    You already have the 8-page type reports as a first-session bonus — that's gold. Consider adding: first-time discounts, limited-time offers ("first 5 bookings"), dollars-off for booking within a certain window. Limited-time framing gets people off the fence.

A Rule Worth Repeating

You are not teaching tarot. You're using tarot as a tool inside your method. Content should never position you as a tarot educator or offer tarot how-to. Lead with the transformation — self-trust, the override pattern, the moment the answer arrives — and name The Pattern Beneath Method as much as you can. The method is what's unique to you.

Ideal Client & Freebie Strategy

Copy the language from here into your posts, emails, and About page. It's already dialed in.

The Self-Aware Overthinker

Introspective woman who has done the work — therapy, books, assessments, journaling — and can explain exactly why she behaves the way she does. But when it's time to decide, set a boundary, or admit what she wants, that self-awareness stops translating into action. She already knows her answer. She just distrusts it the moment it arrives.

Her real struggle isn't finding the answer. It's trusting herself enough to act on what she already knows.

Readiness signals (her exact words)

"I know what I want, so why can't I choose it?" "I've analyzed this from every angle and I'm still in the same place." "I don't need another explanation. I need to trust myself."

What she needs from you

A space to hear herself clearly, plus evidence that her inner knowing is reliable. Enneagram gives language for the mechanism of self-abandonment. Tarot slows the moment enough for her own response to emerge before the override takes control.

Your freebie: Discover Your Self-Trust Language

Tagline: Find the key that helps you hear your inner knowing and move forward with greater confidence.

Five results — Inner Clarity, Embodied Knowing, Personal Evidence, Values Alignment, Supported Reflection. Each reveals a doorway back to the reader's own knowing without diagnosing her as a fixed personality type. The natural next question becomes: "I know how my self-trust speaks, so why do I keep overriding it?" — which leads to booking the typing session.
